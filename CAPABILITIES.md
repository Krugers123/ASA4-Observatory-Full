# ASA4 Observatory Full Edition Capabilities

ASA4 Observatory Full Edition is an extended private research and operator console for trajectory-level Human-AI observability.

## Public-Safe Capability Summary

The full private edition supports:

- live trajectory observation
- semantic drift monitoring
- threshold pressure visibility
- coherence and complementarity tracking
- semantic envelope analysis
- protocol-layer operator readouts
- session-level forensic review
- multi-session pattern analysis
- trajectory compression into compact signatures
- report generation for operator review
- audit-oriented session inspection
- visual research dashboards

## What It Studies

ASA4 studies whether a Human-AI interaction remains stable across time.

It focuses on the trajectory rather than a single answer:

- does the anchor remain visible?
- does meaning narrow too early?
- does coherence remain recoverable?
- does pressure accumulate across turns?
- do patterns repeat across sessions?
- does the operator still have enough information to intervene?

## Operator-Facing Emphasis

The full edition is designed to make trajectory instability visible to a human operator.

It is not a fully autonomous intervention system.

It is a research and observability surface intended to support:

- detection
- review
- escalation
- documentation
- controlled interpretation

## Relation To The Public ASA Observatory

The public ASA Observatory repository demonstrates a working installable system with a narrower public scope.

ASA4 Observatory Full Edition is broader and more operator-rich. It includes additional private research surfaces, protocol-layer views, report generation, and full dashboard navigation.
